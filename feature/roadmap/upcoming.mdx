# Upcoming Features

This roadmap outlines the features and improvements we're currently working on for future releases. While we strive to deliver these enhancements on schedule, development priorities may shift based on user feedback and market conditions.

## Q3 2023 (July-September)

### Machine Learning Attribution Model

**Planned Release: August 2023**

We're developing an advanced attribution model powered by machine learning algorithms to provide more accurate insights into your marketing effectiveness.

-   Probabilistic attribution across fragmented user journeys
-   Adaptive learning from your unique conversion patterns
-   Predictive analytics for campaign optimization
-   Custom attribution window settings

### Expanded Integrations Ecosystem

**Planned Release: September 2023**

Connect Linkrunner with more of your marketing stack:

-   **New Ad Platform Integrations**:
    -   TikTok Ads
    -   Pinterest Ads
    -   Reddit Ads
-   **Analytics Tools**:
    -   Amplitude
    -   Mixpanel
    -   Segment
-   **CRM Systems**:
    -   Salesforce
    -   HubSpot
    -   Zoho CRM

### Real-Time Analytics Dashboard

**Planned Release: Late September 2023**

Track campaign performance as it happens:

-   Live data streaming with sub-minute latency
-   Real-time conversion tracking
-   Instant attribution updates
-   Automated alerts for campaign anomalies
-   Performance threshold notifications

## Q4 2023 (October-December)

### Enhanced Privacy Controls

**Planned Release: October 2023**

Stay ahead of privacy regulations:

-   GDPR and CCPA compliance tooling
-   Data anonymization options
-   Consent management integration
-   Privacy-first attribution methods
-   User data retention controls

### Predictive Campaign Optimization

**Planned Release: November 2023**

Let AI help you maximize campaign performance:

-   Budget allocation recommendations
-   Creative performance predictions
-   Audience targeting suggestions
-   Optimal campaign timing insights
-   Automated bid adjustments

### Advanced A/B Testing Framework

**Planned Release: December 2023**

Scientifically test and optimize your marketing:

-   Multivariate testing capabilities
-   Statistical significance calculator
-   Audience segmentation for tests
-   Visual results reporting
-   Automated winner implementation

## 2024 Preview

Here's an early look at some major initiatives planned for early 2024:

-   **Self-Service Campaign Builder**: Create, launch, and monitor marketing campaigns directly within Linkrunner
-   **Custom Marketing Intelligence**: Build personalized analytics views with a no-code interface
-   **Cross-App Attribution**: Track user journeys across multiple apps within your portfolio
-   **AI-Powered Content Recommendations**: Get suggestions for ad creative and messaging
-   **Enterprise Data Warehouse Connectors**: Direct integrations with Snowflake, BigQuery, and Redshift
